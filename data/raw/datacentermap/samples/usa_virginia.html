Virginia Data Center Market
The data centers in Virginia are primarily located in Northern Virginia. To many, Northern Virginia is considered the data center capital of the world, as it is considered the largest data center market not only in the US or the US east coast, but globally.

The prominent position of Northern Virginia as the data center hub on the US east coast, is due to presence of many cloud operators and networks. The Northern Virginia data center market is very well connected and the point of exchange for a lot of the US internet traffic.

The primary concentration of data centers in Northern Virginia is in and around Ashburn, Reston, Sterling, Chantilly, Manassas, Herndon, Vienna and Leesburg in/aroudn Loudoun, Fairfax and William Counties in Northern Virginia.
